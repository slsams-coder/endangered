Endangered Animals

Simple English for learners

Sea Lion

  • Size: 2–2.5 meters
  • Diet: Fish and squid
  • Habitat: Ocean coasts
  • Special: Very playful
  • Threats: Pollution, fishing nets

Asian Elephant

  • Size: Up to 3 meters tall
  • Diet: Plants
  • Habitat: Forests in Asia
  • Special: Uses trunk like a hand
  • Threats: Habitat loss, poaching

Orangutan

  • Size: 1.2–1.5 meters
  • Diet: Fruit and insects
  • Habitat: Rainforests
  • Special: Very intelligent
  • Threats: Deforestation, illegal trade

Snow Leopard

  • Size: 1–1.3 meters
  • Diet: Meat
  • Habitat: Cold mountains
  • Special: Thick fur for cold weather
  • Threats: Hunting, climate change

Black Rhino

  • Size: Up to 1.6 meters tall
  • Diet: Plants
  • Habitat: Africa
  • Special: Strong horn
  • Threats: Poaching, habitat loss

Bluefin Tuna

  • Size: Up to 4 meters
  • Diet: Fish
  • Habitat: Open ocean
  • Special: Very fast swimmer
  • Threats: Overfishing, illegal fishing

Blue Whale

  • Size: Up to 30 meters
  • Diet: Krill
  • Habitat: Ocean
  • Special: Largest animal on Earth
  • Threats: Ship strikes, noise pollution

Polar Bear

  • Size: Up to 3 meters
  • Diet: Seals
  • Habitat: Arctic
  • Special: Strong swimmer
  • Threats: Melting ice, climate change
